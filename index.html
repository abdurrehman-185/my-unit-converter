Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Is 1 GB equal to 1000 or 1024 MB?

In computing (Binary standard), 1 GB equals 1,024 MB. Some manufacturers use the decimal standard (1,000 MB), but SimplyConvert Pro uses the 1,024 standard for precision.

How do I convert Decimal to Hex?

Select "Number Bases," set From to Decimal and To to Hex. Hexadecimal uses numbers 0-9 and letters A-F to represent values.
